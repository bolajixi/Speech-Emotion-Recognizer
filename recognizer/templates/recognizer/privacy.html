Privacy.

Privacy is a fundamental human right. With Recognize, it’s one core value. Recognize was designed from the beginning to identify your emotion without knowing your identity. You don’t sign-in or sign-up with your email. Your voice is your data and we believe it's up to you to share and with whom you share with.

When Recognize does process or store data on our servers, it’s associated with a random identifier — a string of letters and numbers. That data is used identify your emotion and deleted immediately after processing.
